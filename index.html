H&G Armory - Heroes & Generals Weapon Stats

H&G Armory is a free weapon statistics database and comparison tool for the WWII shooter Heroes & Generals. Every value is computed from the game's datamined values, covering damage and damage falloff, damage per second, hits to kill, time to kill, rate of fire, muzzle velocity, effective range, cone of fire, recoil, sway, reload time, armor damage, armor penetration and credit costs.

All infantry equipment is included for the US, German and Soviet factions: rifles and bolt-action rifles, sub machine guns, light machine guns, handguns, anti-tank launchers such as the Bazooka, Panzerschreck and Panzerfaust, plus grenades, mines and other gear. Loadouts can be configured with attachments, alternative ammunition types and the Fast Reload, Iron Fist, Heavy Set and Grenadier badges, and any two or three weapons can be compared side by side.
